Income Tax Guide & FAQ (FY 2025-26)

Old vs New Tax Regime: Kaunsa Hai Behtar?

India mein taxpayers ke paas do options hain. Lekin aksar log confuse rehte hain ki unhe kis regime mein zyada bachat hogi.

  • New Tax Regime: Isme tax rates kam hain aur Standard Deduction ₹75,000 hai, lekin 80C jaise exemptions nahi milte.
  • Old Tax Regime: Agar aap HRA, LIC, aur PPF mein invest karte hain, toh ye aapke liye best ho sakta hai.

How to Save Maximum Tax?

Sirf kamana kafi nahi hai, Tax Saving bhi zaroori hai. Aap Section 80C (₹1.5L), 80D (Health Insurance), aur NPS (Extra ₹50k) ka use karke apni tax liability zero kar sakte hain.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Standard Deduction kitna hai? ▼

New Regime mein Standard Deduction ₹75,000 hai aur Old Regime mein ₹50,000 hai.

Kya main har saal regime badal sakta hoon? ▼

Haan, agar aap salaried employee hain toh aap har saal switch kar sakte hain.

ELSS mein invest karna kyun zaroori hai? ▼

ELSS ka lock-in period sirf 3 saal hai aur ye 80C mein sabse zyada returns dene wala option hai.
