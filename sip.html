SIP Kya Hai aur Ye Kaise Kaam Karta Hai?

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) mutual funds mein invest karne ka ek anushasit (disciplined) tarika hai. Isme aap har mahine ek fixed amount (jaise ₹500 ya ₹5000) invest karte hain.

Hamara SIP calculator aapko batata hai ki long-term mein Power of Compounding kaise kaam karti hai aur kaise chote investments ek bada corpus ban sakte hain.

Power of Compounding ka Jaadu ✨

Agar aap ₹5,000 mahina 20 saal tak invest karte hain (12% return par), toh aapka invested amount ₹12 Lakh hoga, lekin uski total value lagbhag ₹50 Lakh tak pahunch sakti hai!

SIP Investment Tips

  • ✔ Early Start: Jitna jaldi shuru karenge, compounding ka utna zyada fayda milega.
  • ✔ Consistency: Market upar ho ya niche, apni SIP kabhi band na karein.
  • ✔ Step-up SIP: Har saal apni income ke saath SIP amount thoda badhayein.

Common FAQs

Kya SIP safe hai?

SIP market-linked hoti hai, isliye risk hota hai, lekin long-term (5-10 saal) mein ye FD se kaafi behtar returns de sakti hai.

Minimum kitna invest kar sakte hain?

Zyadatar mutual funds mein aap ₹500 mahina se SIP shuru kar sakte hain.
